## **SECTION 7: CLOSING BEST PRACTICES**

### **The 70/30 Talking Rule**

**During demos:** You should talk only 30% of the time. Let them talk 70%.

- Ask questions: "How do you currently handle...?"
- Listen: Take notes
- Adapt pitch to their specific pain point
- They'll sell themselves

---

### **Always Have a Next Step**

**Never end a call without A SPECIFIC NEXT STEP:**

❌ *Bad:* "Let's stay in touch."

✅ *Good:* "I'll send you a trial link by EOD today. You'll be live by Wednesday. I'll call you Thursday to check in."

---

### **Social Proof Closing**

As you get customers, use them:

"I just helped [Company Name] (you probably know them) implement this on their [Project X]. In 30 days, they tracked [number] workers with 99% accuracy. Their PM said it saved them about ₹40K in that month alone."
