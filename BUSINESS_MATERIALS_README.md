## **COMMON QUESTIONS**

### **Q: Should I use the same script for all companies?**

**A:** No. Use the structure, but customize details:
- Company name (research their projects)
- Worker count (based on their size)
- Pain point (specific to their challenge)
- ROI calculation (apply to their numbers)

→ Makes it feel personal, not templated.

---

### **Q: How many times should I follow up?**

**A:** Follow the sequence in SALES_SCRIPT_PRICING.md:
1. Email (Day 1)
2. LinkedIn message (Day 2)
3. WhatsApp (Day 3)
4. Phone call (Day 5)
5. Final reach-out (Day 10)

**After Day 10:** If no response, move on. Don't be annoying.

---

### **Q: What if they ask for a custom quote?**

**A:** Use the pricing models from SALES_SCRIPT_PRICING.md:
- Per-site: ₹9,999
- Per-worker: ₹80/worker
- Hybrid: ₹7,500 + ₹50/worker

→ If they want a discount, offer 30% off for PMC or 50% off for pilot/trial.
