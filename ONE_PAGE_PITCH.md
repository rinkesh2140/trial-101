## **THE PROBLEM**

Construction companies waste **₹30-50K/month per site** on:
- ❌ Manual attendance tracking (fraud-prone)
- ❌ Excel spreadsheets (errors, delays)
- ❌ Supervision overhead (2-3 people per site)
- ❌ Delayed payroll (worker dissatisfaction)
- ❌ No real-time visibility (poor decision-making)

**Current reality:** Supervisors write in notebooks → data gets transcribed to Excel → errors → back-and-forth → 4-5 hours per week wasted.

---

## **THE SOLUTION**

SiteHub is a **mobile-first labor management platform** designed specifically for construction.

**Three apps in one:**
1. **Labor App** — Workers tap IN/OUT (simple, 1 tap)
2. **Supervisor Verification** — Real-time dashboard, approve/reject entries
3. **Project Manager Reports** — Instant reports, analytics, payroll-ready data

## **KEY FEATURES**

✅ Real-time attendance (GPS + mobile)

✅ Instant daily/weekly/monthly reports  
✅ Leave & approval tracking  
✅ Team messaging & coordination
